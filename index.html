About Blossoms

"In the heart of Christ's vibrant hue,
Where talents bloom in a colorful brew,
Art, stage, and words take flight anew,
In Blossoms' embrace, excellence rings true."

Blossoms is the intra-deanery cultural fest at Christ (Deemed To Be University) Bangalore with the motto ‘Be the best’. This is a unique venture where all 7000 students of Christ University, Bangalore participate in a cultural event. The events are divided into four categories - Art, Theatre, Stage & Literary. Students compete in multiple events across these categories. The winners lay their hands on the Inter-Deanery trophy for Artistic Excellence and Service.
