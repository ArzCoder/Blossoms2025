About Blossoms

Blossoms is the intra-deanery cultural fest at Christ (Deemed To Be University) Bangalore with the motto ‘Be the best’. This is a unique venture where all 7000 students of Christ University, Bangalore participate in a cultural event. The events are divided into four categories - Art, Theatre, Stage & Literary. Students compete in multiple events across these categories. The winners lay their hands on the Inter-Deanery trophy for Artistic Excellence and Service.Blossoms serves as a platform for students to explore their hidden talents and gain confidence through participation.

Event Categories

Art

The Art category highlights visual creativity—drawing, painting, design, and craft—inviting participants to translate ideas into compelling visuals. It celebrates originality, composition, color, and technique across traditional and digital mediums.

Literary
