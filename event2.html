Botanical Workshop

April 22, 2025 | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Join our expert horticulturists and floral designers for an immersive hands-on workshop that unlocks the secrets of botanical artistry. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just beginning your journey with plants, this workshop offers something for everyone.

Learn the fundamentals of flower arranging, from selecting the perfect blooms to creating stunning centerpieces and bouquets. Discover plant care techniques, propagation methods, and sustainable gardening practices that will help your green thumb flourish.

Participants will work with fresh flowers, exotic plants, and professional tools under the guidance of our master florists. Take home not only new skills but also beautiful arrangements to decorate your home.

Workshop Schedule

  • 9:00 AM - Welcome & Introduction to Floral Design
  • 10:00 AM - Flower Selection & Preparation
  • 11:30 AM - Basic Arrangement Techniques
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch Break & Networking
  • 2:00 PM - Advanced Techniques & Plant Care
  • 3:30 PM - Q&A Session & Take-Home Tips
