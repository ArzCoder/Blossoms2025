Literary Events – Where minds ignite

Blossoms 2025 | Category: Literary

The Literary category at Blossoms 2025 celebrates the power of words that shape our thoughts and stories. Each event is a stage for expression — from poetry and prose to debate and drama, voices come alive through rhythm, reason, and emotion.Whether through storytelling, speech, or script, this category invites everyone to witness the magic of language and the art of ideas in their most captivating form.

Debate

A battle of ideas where logic, eloquence, and perspective come together to shape powerful arguments.

Extempore

A spontaneous showcase of wit and clarity, where speakers think on their feet and express ideas with confidence and grace.

Air Crash

A creative role-play event where participants imagine surviving a plane crash and use quick thinking, teamwork, and storytelling to make their case for survival.

Dumb Charades

A fun, expressive guessing game where participants act out words or movie titles without speaking, relying only on gestures and imagination.

Just A Minute (JAM)

A one-minute challenge where participants speak on a given topic without hesitation, repetition, or deviation, testing their spontaneity and fluency.

Creative Writing

Adorn hands with intricate patterns that blend tradition, elegance, and artistic grace.

Quiz

A thrilling test of knowledge and speed where participants race to answer questions across diverse topics with accuracy and insight.

Pot Pourri

A lively mix of fun games and challenges that test creativity, wit, and teamwork in a light-hearted, entertaining way.
