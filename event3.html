Garden Tour Experience

May 10, 2025 | 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Embark on a magical journey through some of the most breathtaking gardens in the region. Our expert guides will lead you through meticulously landscaped gardens, hidden botanical treasures, and sustainable green spaces that showcase the harmony between nature and human creativity.

Discover rare and exotic plants, learn about native species, and explore innovative gardening techniques. From serene rose gardens to vibrant butterfly habitats, each stop on the tour offers unique insights into the world of horticulture and environmental stewardship.

This full-day experience includes transportation between gardens, expert commentary, photography opportunities, and a special lunch featuring garden-to-table cuisine. Perfect for nature enthusiasts, photographers, and anyone seeking inspiration for their own gardening projects.

Tour Itinerary

  • 8:00 AM - Departure & Welcome Briefing
  • 9:00 AM - Rose Garden Exploration
  • 11:00 AM - Butterfly Garden & Native Plants
  • 1:00 PM - Garden-to-Table Lunch
  • 2:30 PM - Sustainable Gardens & Water Features
  • 4:30 PM - Q&A and Farewell
  • 5:00 PM - Return to Starting Point
