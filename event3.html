Music Events – Where ideas flourish

Blossoms 2025 | Category: Music

The Music category at Blossoms 2025 celebrates the rhythm and harmony that connect hearts and souls. Each event is a symphony of talent — from soulful vocals to dynamic instrumentals, melodies weave stories that words cannot express. Whether through beats, notes, or tunes, this category invites everyone to feel the pulse of passion and experience the universal language of music in its purest form.

Indian Folk & Film Singing (SOLO)

A soulful celebration of India’s musical heritage, where singers blend tradition and cinema through expressive melodies and powerful vocals.

Western Singing (SOLO)

A vibrant showcase of global melodies and vocal artistry, where singers captivate with rhythm, emotion, and style.

Acoustic Music Group (WESTERN)

A harmonious blend of voices and instruments creating soulful, unplugged melodies that resonate with pure musical energy.

Battle of Bands (WESTERN)

An electrifying face-off where bands ignite the stage with powerful performances, dynamic rhythms, and unmatched musical energy.

Battle of Bands (INDIAN)

A high-voltage musical showdown where Indian bands fuse tradition and modernity, delivering powerful rhythms and captivating performances.
