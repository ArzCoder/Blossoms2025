Floral Fantasy Exhibition

March 15, 2025 | 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Step into a world of wonder at our Floral Fantasy Exhibition, where nature's most exquisite creations come to life. This immersive experience features stunning arrangements of exotic flowers from around the globe, carefully curated by master florists and botanical artists.

Discover rare orchids, vibrant tulips, delicate roses, and innovative floral installations that blend art and nature seamlessly. Each display tells a story, from the serene beauty of cherry blossoms to the dramatic flair of protea arrangements.

Perfect for photography enthusiasts, nature lovers, and anyone seeking inspiration, this exhibition promises to captivate your senses and ignite your imagination.

Event Schedule

  • 10:00 AM - Opening Ceremony & Ribbon Cutting
  • 11:00 AM - Guided Tours (Every hour)
  • 1:00 PM - Floral Arrangement Demonstration
  • 3:00 PM - Photography Workshop
  • 5:00 PM - Closing Remarks & Networking
