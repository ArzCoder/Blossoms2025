About Blossoms

"In the heart of Christ's vibrant hue, where talents bloom in a colorful brew..."

Our Mission

To provide a dynamic platform for students of the School of Sciences to showcase their diverse talents, foster a spirit of healthy competition, and cultivate a vibrant campus culture rooted in creativity and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most anticipated and inclusive cultural fest, nurturing the next generation of artists, performers, and leaders by encouraging participation, celebrating diversity, and promoting artistic expression in all its forms.

A Legacy of Excellence

2018 - The Genesis

Blossoms was first conceptualized as a small-scale intra-departmental event. The first fest saw 500+ participants in 10 events.

2020 - Going Digital

In response to global challenges, Blossoms successfully transitioned to a fully online format, engaging over 3,000 students from their homes.

2023 - The Grand Return

The fest returned to campus with unprecedented scale, featuring 50+ events across all categories and participation from all 7,000 students.

2025 - Brighter Blooms

This year, Blossoms aims to 'Be the Best' with new exciting categories, professional workshops, and a focus on service and artistic excellence.
